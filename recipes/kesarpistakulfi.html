Kesar Pista Kulfi

Description

Kesar Pista Kulfi is an ultimate bliss for every sweet lover. Made with some simple ingredients, anyone can make this traditional delight. Filled with the goodness of Kesar and prepared with milk and pistachios. Ideally, sugar is added to the Kesar Pista kulfi to give it a sweet taste, but you can also add condensed milk to make it more creamy.
